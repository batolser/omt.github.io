Описание категории

Plugs for painting. High temperature masking and protection for all painting and coating processes. Plugs are made in stabilized Siliconewith special blends, EPDM, Neoprene, Rubber NBR, SBR, ecc. and are reusable many times, with shapes suitable for many processes and several diametersi. Bespoke molds: We supply bespoke shapes plugs in short time; without minimum order.
